The Problem with Trial Matching Today

Clinical trial recruitment struggles because eligible patients often never hear about available studies.

Complex Eligibility

Protocol eligibility criteria are lengthy and difficult to review quickly—often 20+ pages of complex rules.

No Smart Matching

Systems list trials but don't automatically compare patient data to trial needs. Coordinators must manually extract rules.

Black Box AI

Existing AI tools don't explain how matching decisions are made, limiting clinical trust and adoption.

How TrialMatch Works

From patient profile to trial recommendation in minutes—with clear explanations at every step.

01
Eligibility Intelligence

We structure trial eligibility criteria from protocol documents into clear, machine-readable rules. No manual extraction needed.

02
Patient Profile Match

Enter or import patient clinical data—diagnosis, biomarkers, labs, and treatment history. Our engine compares against all trials.

03
Explainable Results

See why a patient qualifies or doesn't—every criterion evaluated with clear patient vs. required value comparisons.
